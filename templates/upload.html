Convolution Matrix Anomaly Detection (CMAD)

Antarctic Anomalous Melting Detection Tool

Upload two daily satellite images of the entire Antarctic region obtained from NOAA satellite data.

The CMAD system analyzes changes between these two images and automatically highlights areas experiencing anomalous melting.

Please ensure that:

  • Both images cover the full Antarctic region
  • Images represent two different dates
  • Day t is the target day
  • Day t-n is any earlier baseline day used for comparison
The system compares Day t with Day t-n to detect unusual melt patterns.

Upload Time Period 1 (Day t-n)

Select an earlier satellite image to use as the baseline for comparison (any previous day before the target day).

Upload Time Period 2 (Day t)

Select the satellite image for the target (Day t). CMAD detects anomalous melting by comparing this image with Day t-n.
